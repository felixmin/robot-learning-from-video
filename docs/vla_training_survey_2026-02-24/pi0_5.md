# pi0.5

## Primary sources
- Paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.16054
- Paper (HTML mirror): https://ar5iv.org/html/2504.16054

## Code and implementation
- pi0.5 is presented as an improved post-training recipe over pi0.
- The paper describes transfer and adaptation stages designed to increase robustness and generalization.

## Training configuration (reported)
- Public paper emphasizes recipe structure (transfer + adaptation stages) more than low-level optimizer cards.
- Publicly disclosed values for full recipe knobs (exact learning rate, batch size, total steps, and GPU count) are limited.

## Full vs LoRA
- The reported method is a multi-stage post-training pipeline.
- LoRA is not reported as the primary training method in the paper's main recipe.

## Gaps
- No complete public run-sheet found with all of: exact steps, LR schedule, batch size, and hardware count for the full pi0.5 training pipeline.
